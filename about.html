Derek Saffie is currently a student at Merrimack College in North Andover, MA where he is pursuing his undergraduate degree in Computer Science with a minor in Science and Religion. With a passion for technology and politics, Derek has interned with both Hewlett Packard Enterprise and the New Hampshire GOP. He has also authored several articles for conservative publications. Derek is also a historian, having spent several years researching and chronicling the history of Windham, NH. His work with Windham's history includes a website and the publication of his book, "Historic Tales of Windham" in 2016. Derek currently serves as a trustee of the Windham Museum and as a member of Windham's Historic District Commission/Heritage Commission.
